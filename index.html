The Novel Yard Events

Welcome to The Novel Yard, a beautifully renovated 19th-century event space nestled in the charming village of Agia Varvara, just a short distance from the capital city of Nicosia. Our captivating venue boasts a historic ambiance, featuring a unique internal stone courtyard divided into three sections. This layout creates a versatile and enchanting space suitable for a variety of events.

Step into the lush greenery of our courtyard, a picturesque setting that transforms into a magical paradise by night. Enhanced by dense fairy lights twinkling overhead, these lights cast a warm, inviting glow, creating a mesmerizing backdrop perfect for your special occasions.

At The Novel Yard, we offer more than just a venue - we provide a complete experience. Whether you're planning a baptism, a corporate party, a seminar, a conference, or a fashion event, our adaptable space is meticulously designed to cater to your needs. Our internal courtyard can host up to 200 guests, while our indoor area comfortably accommodates 150 participants, all enveloped in the cozy ambiance of a fireplace.

Strategically located with easy access from major cities, The Novel Yard is your go-to destination for memorable events, exceptional service, and a hint of historical elegance. Join us to forge unforgettable memories in a setting where each detail reflects the enduring sophistication of a bygone era.
